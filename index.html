Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients
Pasta
Eggs
Parmesan cheese
Pancetta or bacon

Details

A classic Italian pasta dish that's simple and delicious.

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Fry the pancetta or bacon.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese.
  4. Mix everything together and serve.

Recipe source: BBCGoodFood
